About This Website

This site is the central hub for the Blaze Production Team. It gives our team quick access to checklists, reference info, tools, and links used before, during, and after services.

How to Use This Site

Checklists: Each production role has its own checklist, info tab, and cheat sheet so every team member can focus on what they are responsible for.

Quick Links: Fast access to common tools like mic assignments, IP directories, and other production resources.

Mobile Friendly: This site is built to be used from a phone during weekend services. You can bookmark it or add it to your home screen for quick access.
