Blaze Production

About This Website

This website was built to help you serve and grow as part of the Blaze Church Production Team. It’s your central hub for training videos, setup and shutdown checklists, quick troubleshooting tips, and other important resources tailored to each role on the team.

How to Use This Site

Resources: Organized by team roles — Audio, Lighting, Producer, Camera Operator, and Hawkeye — each section includes setup, shutdown, quick troubleshooting tips, training videos, and checklists.

Command Center: A hub for web controls tied to our networked equipment. REMEMBER: If you're not connected to the Blaze Admin Wi-Fi, some links may not work due to device names or local IPs.

Quick Links: Fast access to commonly used tools per position, such as Boxcast for Producers or the Mic Assignment tools for Audio.

More: This menu holds extra utilities — like the Settings page, where you can toggle dark or light mode. Your preferences are saved to your device so you won’t need to reset them every time.

Note: This site and its content may be shared with other churches across the island (and beyond!) to help other teams create distraction-free environments for people to meet Jesus. It's just another way we are blazing a path for lost people to know Him.

On mobile devices, the layout shifts to make things easier to tap and find. You can also bookmark this site or add it to your home screen so it works like an app for quick and easy access during weekend services.
